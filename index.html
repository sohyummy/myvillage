My Village

A way to give back to your community

Being a member of the international student community, I observed a lot of the students find it difficult to be a member of their local community. Further research revealed that people are less likely to be a part of their community, so I wanted to research how this problem could be solved.

My Village is a mobile application that allows you to be a bigger part of your community by doing activities. Users get points by doing activities that can be used to unlock fun activities and more neighbourhoods.

Solution
Discover your neighbourhood
You start with understanding the impact of community from you locality. The goal here is to connect people with interesting communal activities such as volunteering, walking dogs, and helping a neighbour.
Personalise your experience
Based on the users need of being able to personalise the experience they choose to have in their community, a preference section was designed in the onboarding experience.
Actions that keep on giving
The game mechanic is build around gaining good karma points. The more you give your community, the more you can get back in return.
